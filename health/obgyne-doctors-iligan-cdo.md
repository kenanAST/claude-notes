## KEY OBSERVATIONS

1. **MFM/Perinatology is the best-covered subspecialty** with 5-6 specialists across the region — patients with high-risk pregnancies have local options
2. **Gynecologic oncology has 3 specialists** (Mercado, Mata-Carrillo, Sanchez) — adequate for the region's needs
3. **Reproductive endocrinology/fertility is critically underserved** — only 2 PSRM-listed doctors (Tingne, Diawatan) for all of Region X
4. **No urogynecologist** was identified in the region — patients needing pelvic reconstructive surgery must travel to Davao or Manila
5. **No robotic gynecologic surgery** is available in the region
6. **Maria Reyna-Xavier University Hospital** has the most diverse OB-GYN subspecialty coverage (MFM, Gyn-Onc, REI, MIS all represented)
7. **Iligan City** has 2 MFM specialists (Boransing, Putol) — unusually strong perinatology coverage for a smaller city
8. **Dr. Boransing (Iligan)** is arguably the single most credentialed OB-GYN in all of Northern Mindanao — Harvard postgrad + quadruple fellowship
