## KEY OBSERVATIONS

1. **5 FPSO-HNS Fellows** in the Iligan-CDO corridor: Drs. Isla (Iligan), Mendoza (CDO), Racmat (CDO), Revilla-Lacandula (CDO), and Mundo (CDO). The Fellow credential is the gold standard — prioritize these doctors for complex cases
2. **No fellowship-trained ENT subspecialist** exists in the region — no dedicated rhinologist, otologist/neurotologist, laryngologist, pediatric ENT, or facial plastic surgeon. All ENTs are general otolaryngologists. For subspecialty care, patients must travel to Davao, Cebu, or Manila
3. **CUMC dominates ENT in CDO** with 6+ consultants, the MENTOR Center (only ENT rehab facility in Northern Mindanao), and co-hosting the region's only accredited ENT residency program
4. **Maria Reyna-XUH has the second-largest ENT department** with 8 EENT-HNS consultants
5. **Iligan City has only 1 verified board-certified ENT doctor** (Dr. Isla) — a critical bottleneck. Two other EENT clinics exist (Tamparong, Layos) but their board certification is unverified. For ENT emergencies when Dr. Isla is unavailable, patients must travel to CDO (~1.5 hours)
6. **Cochlear implants are NOT available** in the region — patients must go to Manila (The Medical City, Makati Medical Center)
7. **No sleep lab (polysomnography)** exists in CDO or Iligan — nearest is Davao, Cebu, or Manila. Dr. Richmond Ceniza at Madonna & Child is the only sleep medicine fellow in N. Mindanao
8. **The MSU-IIT x PSO-HNS 3D printing collaboration** (led by Dr. Isla, with participation from Dr. Racmat and others) is a pioneering initiative that has brought virtual surgical planning technology to Northern Mindanao — generating P4.6M in research funding and training 60+ surgeons nationally
9. **NMMC's accredited ENT residency** (CDO Consortium) is building the region's future ENT workforce — the program has produced multiple board-certified and fellowship-level ENTs already practicing in the corridor
10. **Dr. Barapantao's Marawi hearing center** extends ENT care to the underserved BARMM region — an important regional contribution
